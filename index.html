About the Biz

The steps to amazing refurb'd socks

  1. Locate orphaned socks on the streets of Portland
  2. Wash said socks
  3. Dry said socks
  4. Match said socks appropriately according to size (patterns are irrelevant)
  5. Package socks in sustainable 100% post-consumer reusable hemp bags (Oregon-grown in a bird sanctuary fertilized with vegan bird feces)
  6. Distribute socks by bicycle to local Whole Foods stores
